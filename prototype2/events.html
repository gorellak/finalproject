54th Annual Pioneer Days

August 11-13, 2017

Pioneer Days is a three day event where people come to compete in tractor pulls, show off thier antique tractors and equipment, and finally just enjoy the history of farming pioneer life. With displays from threshing to blackmsmithing there's something for everyone.

Gate Admission:
$7 per day per person Age 10 and under FREE Souvenir buttons available
Have something interesting and historic you want to display like an antique tractor? Become an exhibitor and get in free!

Primitive Camping (no electricity) available first come, first served $8 per day for every day parked on showgrounds

Handicap parking is available on the main showgrounds with People wagon tours around showgrounds.

Crafts and numerous other displays are around the grounds to enjoy.

Flea Market Information


Vendors Wanted
Flea Market - Crafters - Farmers Market - Direct Sales
Any and all vendors welcome!

RATES ARE AS FOLLOWS:
Three Days
13' x 13' three day space $20.00 each space requested
13' x 26' three day space $38.00 each space requested


Booth rates include 2 free gate passes PER vendor. No exceptions!
Primitive camping is free within your flea market space only. Camping on any other part of the show grounds will result in camping fees being charged.

Any time after noon on Thursday or no later than 7:30 a.m. on Friday - Sunday
